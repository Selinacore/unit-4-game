Crystal Collector

How to play:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals to choose from...

When you click on a crystal it will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win by matching your total score with the random number.

You lose by going over the random number.

Each crystal's value is hidden until you click on it. When the game restarts the values reset.
