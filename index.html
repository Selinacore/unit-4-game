Crystal Collector

How to play:

You will be given a random number target at the start of the game.

There are four crystals to click on...

When you click on a crystal it will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win by matching your total score with the random number target.

You lose by going over the random number target.

Each crystal's value is hidden until you click on it. When the game restarts the values reset.
